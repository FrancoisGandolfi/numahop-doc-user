    Documentary Unit​

    Documentary units are accessible from > Documentary Units. The list of units is displayed in the left column. When you click to select a unit, its content is displayed.

    Documentary Unit Detail

    The notice editing area is accessible via the button . Detailed information is presented in tabs.

    Create a Documentary Unit

    A documentary unit is an entity that must always be linked to a batch.

    To access it, click on Documents then select Documentary Units.

    Click the + icon to create a new documentary unit and fill in the label for the unit.

    Fill in the fields of the documentary unit form, indicating which batch and project it should be linked to. Also fill in the PGCN Identifier. It must match the name of the document to be delivered, as it is named on the FTP.

    Fill in the Radical field: it must be identical to the PGCN Identifier field.

    Indicate whether the document should be archived at CINES and whether it can be distributed.

    At this stage, you can configure distribution to Internet Archive or Omeka. If so, enter the names of the Internet Archive and Omeka collections in which the document should be placed after processing.

    Once the form is complete, click Save.
