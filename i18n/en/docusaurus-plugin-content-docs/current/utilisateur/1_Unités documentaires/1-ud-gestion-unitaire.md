## Documentary Unit

# Create a Documentary Unit

A documentary unit is an entity that must always be linked to a batch.

To access it, click on Documents <img
src="https://www.numahop.fr/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/documents-numaHOP.png"
class="alignnone wp-image-134" decoding="async" width="24"
height="29" /> then select Documentary Units.

Click the + icon
<img src="https://www.numahop.fr/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/Ajouter.png"
class="alignnone wp-image-202" loading="lazy" decoding="async"
width="30" height="27" /> to create a new documentary unit and
fill in the label for the unit.

Fill in the fields of the documentary unit form, indicating which batch and project it should be linked to. Also fill in the [PGCN Identifier](https://www.numahop.fr/2022/09/08/glossaire/). It must match the name of the document to be delivered, as it is named on the [FTP](https://www.numahop.fr/2022/10/25/configuration-du-ftp-de-depot-des-fichiers-a-livrer-dans-numahop/).

Fill in the [Radical](https://www.numahop.fr/2022/09/08/glossaire/) field: it must be identical to the PGCN Identifier field.

Indicate whether the document should be [archived at CINES](http://cines) and whether it can be distributed.

At this stage, you can configure distribution to [Internet Archive](https://www.numahop.fr/2022/10/26/configurer-internet-archive/) or [Omeka](https://www.numahop.fr/2022/10/21/configurer-une-sortie-omeka/). If so, enter the names of the Internet Archive and Omeka collections in which the document should be placed after processing.

Once the form is complete, click Save.
